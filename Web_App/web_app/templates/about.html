About SmartBnB

SmartBnB helps new Airbnb hosts navigate the hurdles of listing strategies by suggesting a listing price for their property for a given day. It is a machine-learning platform powered by historical Airbnb data in Chicago, IL. The model uses information related to listings with high review rates, so that the suggested price yields high booking rates as well. Time series analysis of prices is incorporated into the model, such that seasonal, weekly, and holiday-related fluctuations in prices are reflected in the final suggested price. The price breakdown provided by SmartBnB provides users with insight on market behavior, thus helping them make more informed decisions in their listing strategies.
